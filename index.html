Academics

Our curriculum at a glance.

From early years foundation to junior secondary, every stage is designed to build confident, capable learners rooted in Christian values.

Early Years

Creche – Nursery

Play-based Montessori-inspired learning with a focus on sensory development, early literacy, numeracy, and social skills in a loving Christian environment.

Primary

Primary 1 – 6

Nigeria-British blended curriculum covering English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, Christian Religious Studies, ICT, French, and Creative Arts.

Junior Secondary

JSS 1 – 3

Rigorous preparation for BECE/JSSCE with specialist teaching, CBT practice, leadership projects, and vocational exploration across 12 subjects.

Digital Campus

Where ancient wisdom meets modern tools.

From our LMS and CBT engine to D-Class discipleship and the Leadership Hub, every PREPSA module is built to grow the whole child — mind, heart, and spirit.
