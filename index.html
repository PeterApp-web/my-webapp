Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PREPSA.

Find answers to the most common questions parents ask about our Christian digital campus.

PREPSA (Prescious Prime Stars Academy) is a Christian digital campus located at 38 Asalu Avenue, Apete, Obantoko, Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria. We offer education from Creche through Junior Secondary School (JSS3), blending academic excellence with faith-based character formation and modern digital learning tools. Our motto is "Arise and Shine!"

PREPSA welcomes learners from 3 months old (Creche) through 14 years old (JSS3). Our programs include: Creche (3 months – 1 year), Pre-Nursery (1–2 years), Nursery (3–5 years), Primary 1–6 (6–11 years), and Junior Secondary School JSS1–JSS3 (12–14 years). Each stage is carefully designed to meet the developmental needs of every child.

PREPSA follows a Nigeria-British blended curriculum. Our Early Years program uses Montessori-inspired play-based learning. Primary and Junior Secondary levels combine the Nigerian national curriculum with Cambridge-influenced standards. We are WAEC accredited and recognized by the Ogun State Ministry of Education. Subjects include English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, CRS, ICT, French, Creative Arts, and more.

Faith is woven into every aspect of PREPSA life. We hold daily devotions, weekly chapel services, and D-Class discipleship sessions. Biblical principles underpin our character education, leadership training, and community service initiatives. Our goal is to raise servant leaders who love God and serve their generation with integrity and compassion.

To apply, fill out our online application form with the child's full name, date of birth, and the class you're applying for. You can also book a campus tour to experience PREPSA firsthand.
