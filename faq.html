The admission process includes four simple steps:

  1. Submit an application form — online via our Admissions page or at the school office.
  2. Attend an entrance assessment — for Primary and JSS applicants (age‑appropriate).
  3. Receive admission offer — within 5–7 working days after assessment.
  4. Complete registration and fee payment — to secure your child's place.

Applications for the 2026/2027 academic session are now open!

Was this helpful?

Please prepare the following documents:

  • Completed application form
  • Two recent passport photographs of the child
  • Birth certificate or sworn declaration of age
  • Previous school report cards (last 2 academic years)
  • Immunization records (required for Crèche/Nursery)
  • Parent/guardian government‑issued ID
Was this helpful?

Our assessments are designed to be child‑friendly and stress‑free:

  • Crèche & Pre‑Nursery: Interactive play‑based observation session (no written test).
  • Nursery to JSS3: Age‑appropriate assessments in Literacy, Numeracy, and General Knowledge.

The goal is to understand each child's readiness level — not to eliminate candidates. We believe every child can thrive with the right support.

Was this helpful?

We deliver a Nigeria‑British blended curriculum that combines the best of both worlds:

  • Nigerian National Curriculum — ensuring local relevance and compliance with NERDC standards.
  • British best practices — fostering critical thinking, creativity, and global competence.
  • Enrichment: Christian Religious Studies, digital literacy, French, leadership development, and vocational skills.

This blend ensures learners are globally competitive while deeply rooted in Nigerian values and culture.

Was this helpful?
  • Crèche/Nursery: Sensory play, literacy, numeracy, CRS, rhymes, motor skills.
  • Primary: English, Maths, Science, Social Studies, CRS, ICT, French, Agriculture, Creative Arts, Physical Education.
  • Junior Secondary (JSS): Adds Business Studies, Basic Technology, Home Economics, Civic Education, and exam preparation.

A full subject catalogue is available in our academic brochure — request one from the admissions office.

Was this helpful?

We use a balanced assessment model:

  • Continuous Assessment (30%): Quizzes, projects, classwork, homework, and participation.
  • End‑of‑Term CBT Examinations (70%): Computer‑based testing for Primary 3 and above.
  • JSS3: Students sit for the BECE/JSSCE (Basic Education Certificate Examination).

Comprehensive termly reports are sent to parents, followed by parent‑teacher conferences each term.

Was this helpful?

Our current fee structure (per term):

  • Crèche / Pre‑Nursery: ₦150,000
  • Nursery / Primary: ₦180,000
  • Junior Secondary (JSS): ₦200,000

Flexible payment options: Termly payment plans available. Sibling discounts: 10% off for the second child, 15% off for the third. Fees exclude uniforms, textbooks, and external examination charges.

Was this helpful?

Yes! We believe financial circumstances should not prevent access to quality Christian education:

  • Merit‑Based Academic Scholarships: For outstanding students who excel in our entrance assessments.
  • Need‑Based Financial Aid: Available for families who demonstrate genuine financial need.

Contact our admissions office for application forms, requirements, and deadline information.

Yes, PREPSA is a Christ‑centred school. Our faith is woven into daily life:
