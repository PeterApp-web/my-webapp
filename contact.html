FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting us and visiting the school.

What are the best times to visit the school?

We recommend scheduling a visit between 9:00 AM and 1:00 PM on weekdays. Please call ahead to book a tour with our admissions team so we can give you a personalized experience.

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our Admissions page, or pick up a physical form from the school office. Our team will guide you through the entire process.

Do you offer virtual tours for out-of-town families?

Yes. We offer live video tours via WhatsApp or Zoom. Contact our admissions office at presciousprimestars@gmail.com to schedule a convenient time.

What is the school's typical response time?

We typically respond to emails and messages within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, please call our phone line directly at +234 913 257 7502.
