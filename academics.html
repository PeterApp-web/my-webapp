Our Approach

Rigorous, Relevant, Christ‑Centred

We blend the Nigerian national curriculum with British best practices, integrating faith, digital literacy, and leadership at every stage.

Academic Rigour

Learners master core subjects with critical thinking, problem-solving, and inquiry-based methods tailored to each stage.

Christian Worldview

Every subject is taught through the lens of biblical truth, with daily devotion, discipleship, and character formation.

Digital Integration

LMS, CBT exams, coding classes, and digital citizenship prepare learners for the future workforce.

Learning Pathways

Every Stage, Intentional Design

From foundational years to junior secondary, each stage builds on the last — academically, spiritually, and socially.

Creche & Pre-Nursery (3 months – 2 years)

A nurturing, play‑based environment that promotes sensory exploration, early communication, and social-emotional growth in a safe, Christian atmosphere.

Sensory PlayMusic & RhymesBiblical Stories

Nursery (3 – 5 years)

Montessori-inspired early years foundation: literacy, numeracy, phonics, creative arts, and Christian living. Learners build confidence and curiosity.

Early LiteracyNumeracyCRS

Primary 1 – 6 (6 – 11 years)

Nigeria-British blended curriculum. Core subjects include English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, CRS, ICT, French, Agricultural Science, Creative Arts, and Physical Education. Leadership clubs begin.

EnglishMathematicsScienceICT

JSS 1 – 3 (12 – 14 years)

Comprehensive preparation for BECE/JSSCE. Subjects: English, Mathematics, Basic Science, Basic Technology, Social Studies, CRS, Business Studies, French, ICT, Agricultural Science, Home Economics, Civic Education, and Cultural & Creative Arts. Plus Leadership Academy and career guidance.

CBT PracticeLeadership ProjectsMentorship
Digital Campus

Technology-Enhanced Learning

Our digital ecosystem makes learning engaging, accessible, and data-driven — while safeguarding Christian values.

  • Learning Management System (LMS) for assignments, resources, and progress tracking
  • Computer‑Based Testing (CBT) for exams and continuous assessment
  • Digital library and research tools
  • Parent portal for real-time updates on attendance, grades, and announcements
Assessment

Measuring Growth, Not Just Grades

We use continuous, formative, and summative assessments to celebrate progress and identify areas for support.

📝

Continuous Assessment

Weekly quizzes, projects, and class participation (30% of term grade).

💻

CBT Exams

End-of-term computer-based tests (70% of term grade).

📊

Parent Conferences

Termly meetings to discuss learner progress and set goals.
