Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PREPSA, our blog, and Christian education.

What is PREPSA and where is it located?

PREPSA (Prescious Prime Stars Academy) is a Christian digital campus located at 38 Asalu Avenue, Apete, Obantoko, Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria. We offer a Nigeria-British blended curriculum from early years through secondary school, combining academic rigour with strong Christian character formation and digital innovation.

How can I enroll my child at PREPSA?

Visit our Admissions page to complete the online application form, review our entry requirements, and schedule an assessment. You can also call us at +2349132577502 or email presciousprimestars@gmail.com for personalised guidance.

Does PREPSA offer online or hybrid learning options?

Yes! As a digital campus, PREPSA integrates technology into everyday learning. We offer computer-based testing (CBT), digital resources, and blended learning approaches that prepare students for the modern world while maintaining in-person classroom instruction at our Abeokuta campus.

What makes PREPSA different from other schools in Nigeria?

PREPSA stands out through our Nigeria-British blended curriculum, dedicated Christian character formation, Leadership Hub programme, CBT examination centre, and our philosophy of nurturing "purpose-driven children." We balance academic excellence with spiritual growth and digital fluency — all within a supportive, values-rich environment.

How often is the PREPSA blog updated?

We publish new articles 2-4 times per month, covering parenting tips, educational insights, faith-based character building, digital learning trends, and updates from our school community. Subscribe to our newsletter (below) to never miss a post!

What age groups does PREPSA serve?

We serve learners from early years (age 3) through secondary school (age 16+). Our programmes include Crèche/Preschool, Primary, and Secondary divisions — each tailored to the developmental needs of the child while maintaining our core values of faith, excellence, and leadership.
