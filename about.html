Our Story

A Journey of Faith, Learning and Leadership

Since 2009, PREPSA has served families in Abeokuta and beyond with a singular vision—to nurture children academically, spiritually, socially and emotionally while preparing them for meaningful impact in a rapidly changing world.

What began as a modest learning centre has evolved into a vibrant Christian digital campus where innovation meets character, and excellence meets purpose.

Our Journey

Milestones Through The Years

2009

PREPSA was established with a vision to nurture future leaders through quality Christian education.

2020

Digital transformation launched — hybrid classrooms, LMS integration, and CBT readiness.

Today

Raising global leaders with excellence, innovation, character, and an unshakeable Christian foundation.

Learning Pathways

Every Stage Matters

Our programmes are intentionally designed to meet learners at every developmental stage.

Creche

A nurturing environment providing safety, care and early stimulation for infants and toddlers aged 3 months to 1 year.

Pre-Nursery

Exploration, play-based learning and social development that build confidence and curiosity in ages 1–2.

Nursery

Foundational literacy, numeracy and character formation through engaging Montessori-inspired experiences.

Basic Classes

Strong academic foundations, creativity, leadership and life skills development for ages 6–11.

JSS 1–3

Academic preparation, leadership training and digital skills for future success in ages 12–14.
