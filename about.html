Our Story

A Journey of Faith, Learning and Leadership

Since 2009, PREPSA has served families in Abeokuta and beyond with a singular vision—to nurture children academically, spiritually, socially and emotionally while preparing them for meaningful impact in a rapidly changing world.

What began as a modest learning centre has evolved into a vibrant Christian digital campus where innovation meets character, and excellence meets purpose.

Questions Parents Ask

Find answers to the most common questions about PREPSA.

PREPSA offers Creche (3 months – 1 year), Pre-Nursery (1–2 years), Nursery (3–5 years), Basic Classes / Primary (6–11 years), and Junior Secondary School JSS 1–3 (12–14 years). Each stage is carefully designed to meet the developmental needs of every child.

PREPSA is located at 38 Asalu Avenue, Apete, Obantoko, Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria. We serve families across Abeokuta and surrounding communities with a safe, accessible campus.
